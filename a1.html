Assignment 1
Laser-cutting
Design
I started this assignment by thinking something really simple but also flexible and modular. As a basic floral panel, it can be slotted into a connector that allows modularized construction.
The geometry of the floral panel is determined by golden ratio. I set the diameter of the circle as 1 inch and duplicated it on the middle point of the sides of a square with 0.618-inch sides.
For the connector, the outer rim was set to a quarter arc of 1-inch radius and the inner rim was set to the 0.618-inch position of the radius, giving the thickness of the connection as 0.382 inch.
Measure and test
I used a caliper to measure my cardboard and got thickness: 0.10 in. Then I decided to test the fit with three different slot width settings— width: 0.09 in, width: 0.10 in, and width: 0.11 in.
The 0.11-in slot was too loose when connecting parts, and the 0.10 slot was slightly wobbly when connecting more than 3 parts, so I decided to go with width: 0.09 in for the safest connection.
Iterate
The biggest mistake I made was starting to build without testing more parts being connected. Currently the depth of the slot was set to 0.10 in, and due to the shallow connection, the whole model started to fall apart after constructing 10+ pieces. Another issue was because of the tightness of the connection, it required huge pressure to assemble them which could cause broke and chipped piece due to the materiality of cardboard. I only noticed these problems after printing 30+ pieces…
I then iterated the design to set a deeper slot with depth: 0.20 in and create a chamfer: 0.025 in on the connectors. They both worked very well by adding structural stability and ease to assemble.
Final model
I printed over 50+ pieces and assembled some of them as connected spheres.
Laser cutter settings
I initially used the recommended settings from the class, but due to the thickness and a slight bend of my cardboard, some of the pieces were not cut through. So I decided to set a lower speed and it worked well.
The finall laser cutter settings are:speed: 20%, power: 100%, and freq.: 50%
